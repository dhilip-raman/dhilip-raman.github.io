My Resume

Center for Neurogenomics and Clinical Research (CNCR)

Research Assistant, Complex Trait Genetics (Nov 2022 – Present)

  • Investigate disease traits through functional annotation of multiple GWAS, using FUMA, to identify risk SNPs
  • Link disease traits to risk genes through correlation of risk SNPs to genes, using both MAGMA and LD score regression methods
  • Identify cell types associated with disease traits using three cell type-association tools: FUMA’s Cell Type tool, CELLECT, and SC-linker
  • Compare these GWAS annotation methods through statistical analysis of Z-scores
  • Present updates for my computational analysis in department-wide seminars
  • Write a thesis containing a summary of my results that can be presented for a general biology audience
